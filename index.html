The Proposal

求婚故事

For six whole months, Roger kept the ring tucked away, quietly waiting for the perfect moment. On a trip to Las Vegas with friends, he brought Charlotte back to The Strat, a spot filled with memories from an earlier trip they’d taken together. Standing there again, high above the city, he surprised her with a proposal.

整整六个月，浩然一直把戒指珍藏着，默默等待着那个最完美的时刻。 在一次与朋友们前往拉斯维加斯的旅行中，他带驰回到了 The Strat——一个承载着他们此前共同旅行回忆的地方。 再次站在城市之上的高处，他出其不意地向她求婚了。
